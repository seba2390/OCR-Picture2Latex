\abstract{We present a method to combine the all-order treatment of the
  \HIGHEJ exclusive partonic Monte Carlo (\HEJ) with the parton shower of \py, while
  retaining the logarithmic accuracy of both. This procedure enables 
  the generation of fully realistic and hadronised events with \HEJ. 
  Furthermore, the
  combination of the two all-order treatments leads to improvements in the
  quality of the description of observables, in particular for regions with
  disparate transverse scales.%  over what is achieved within each framework
  % separately.
}
